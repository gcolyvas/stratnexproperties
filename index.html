Client Insights

Frequently asked questions about real estate in Tanzania.

Can foreigners buy land or property in Tanzania? +

Yes. Foreigners can acquire land and property under TIC (Tanzania Investment Centre) incentives, which provide guarantees against nationalization and tax holidays. However, land title (Right of Occupancy vs. Leasehold) must be carefully reviewed, a service StratNex provides.

How does the rental market work for executives? +

The executive rental market is dollar-denominated. Leases typically require a security deposit of 2 months rent. StratNex verifies all properties to ensure diplomatic standards of security and proximity to international schools.

Do you handle mining license acquisitions? +

We facilitate the sourcing of mining claims and guide investors through the application process for PL (Prospecting) and ML (Mining) licenses. We ensure compliance with the Ministry of Minerals environmental and social requirements.
