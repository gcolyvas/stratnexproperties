Industrial Godowns & Warehousing

We specialize in the strategic sourcing of high-capacity **Industrial Godowns in Dar es Salaam**, specifically within the Kurasini and Kigamboni port zones. Our mandates include secure warehousing for logistics, manufacturing hubs, and Export Processing Zone (EPZ) development sites.

Commercial Grade-A Mandates

Connecting global capital with **Grade-A Office Spaces in Dar es Salaam**. We handle mandates for commercial land acquisition in the CBD, providing institutional investors with vetted sites for retail developments and corporate headquarters.

Diplomatic & Executive Housing

Managing high-security residential portfolios for international missions. Our expertise covers **Luxury Properties in Masaki and Oysterbay**, offering stand-alone villas and penthouses specifically designed for diplomatic protocols and executive standards.
